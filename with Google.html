Proposal: AI-powered Coordinated Advertising Technology

We propose a collaboration with Google to develop an innovative advertising technology that mimics the style of popular YouTubers' outfits.
Coordinated Advertising Technology

Our AI-powered technology enables advertisers to create ads that resemble the wardrobes of popular YouTubers. By analyzing their fashion choices, our system generates visually similar ads that resonate with the target audience.
An AI-powered fashion style recommendation solution that reflects national trends.
Identified limitations of existing AI style recommendation solutions in the market through collaboration with Korean fashion companies and academia. The problem was the confirmation bias of AI models (data pattern learning), which prevented the recommendation of trendy styles. This issue was resolved using a self-developed AI and has been patented.
